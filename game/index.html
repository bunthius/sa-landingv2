The Game

Star Atlas offers multiple ways to experience the Galia Expanse. Explore on foot in third-person, command vast fleets from a strategic view, or dive into the economy. Choose your path and shape the galaxy's future.

Third-Person Experience

Immerse yourself in AAA-quality exploration. Walk the decks of your ships, engage in FPS combat, and experience Galia up close.

  • Ship interior exploration
  • FPS and ship-to-ship combat
  • Resource extraction missions
  • Quest-driven storylines

Fleet Command RTS

Switch to a top-down strategic view to manage your entire armada. Coordinate fleet movements, control territories, and outmaneuver rival factions.

  • Mass fleet management
  • Territory control
  • Faction warfare
  • Real-time strategy

Browser-Based Play

Access Star Atlas from any device. SAGE Labs and Holosim bring the economy and fleet simulation to your browser. No downloads required.

  • Play anywhere
  • No special hardware needed
  • Full economic participation
  • Free-to-play options
