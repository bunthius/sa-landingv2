The Galia Expanse

The Galia Expanse is a vast, uncharted region teeming with opportunities and dangers. From resource-rich asteroid fields to mysterious anomalies, Galia serves as the primary battleground where MUD, ONI, and Ustur factions compete for dominance.

The Cataclysm

Long ago, the rogue planet Iris collided with seven child planets, creating 'The Cataclysm.' Dense with invaluable materials and radiating mysterious energy, this catastrophic event birthed a unique form of free energy and the most coveted resource zone in the galaxy. The Tufa, enigmatic hybrid mineral-organic beings, now guard the asteroid belts near Iris with zealous intensity.

Getting Started in Galia

  1. Choose your faction alignment (MUD, ONI, or Ustur)
  2. Acquire your first ship via the Galactic Marketplace
  3. Complete introductory missions to learn navigation
  4. Begin resource extraction to fund your expansion
  5. Join a DAC (guild) for collaborative gameplay
