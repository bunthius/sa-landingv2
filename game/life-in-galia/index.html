Life in Galia

The Galia Expanse is home to an extraordinary diversity of life. Three major factions, each comprising multiple species with unique cultures and technologies, compete and cooperate in shaping the galaxy's future.

The Council of Peace

After the devastating Convergence War, the surviving factions established the Council of Peace, a fragile alliance that maintains order in Galia. Each faction brings its own history, strengths, and vision for the future, creating a rich tapestry of politics, culture, and conflict.

MUD (Manus Ultima Divina)

The human faction, originating from Earth. Under the leadership of Charon Gotti Jr., MUD has transformed from its controversial origins into an industrial powerhouse built on fair trade and diplomatic relations.

  • Human-centric culture
  • Industrial strength

ONI Consortium

A coalition of diverse alien species united by shared interests. ONI represents technological advancement and the pursuit of knowledge across the stars.

  • Multi-species alliance
  • Advanced technology
  • Scientific focus
  • Exploration driven

Ustur Sector

A faction of sentient androids guided by logic and efficiency. The Ustur seek to optimize existence itself, bringing a unique perspective to galactic affairs.

  • Android civilization
  • Logic-based society
  • Efficiency focused
  • Technological mastery

The Tufa

Mysterious hybrid mineral-organic beings dwelling near the Cataclysm zone. The Tufa guard the galaxy's most valuable resources with zealous intensity.

  • Ancient beings
  • Mineral-organic hybrid
  • Resource guardians
  • Mysterious origins
