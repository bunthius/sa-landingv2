Fleet Command

Fleet Command transforms Star Atlas into a grand strategy experience. Currently in development. Shift from third-person exploration to a strategic overview where you'll coordinate armadas, control territory, and shape Galia's political landscape.

The Art of Command

In the vastness of Galia, individual heroism only goes so far. The great powers of the galaxy are built by those who can coordinate fleets of hundreds of ships, manage supply lines across light-years, and outthink their opponents before the first shot is fired.
